Details about comparing music services
Amazon Prime Music
Ad-free music streaming with a $99 annual Amazon Prime subscription
Apple Music
Basic service is free. Paid service costs $9.99 a month. Family account is $14.99 for up to six users
Google Play Music
Basic service with ads is free. Ad-free version (Unlimited Google Play) is available for $9.99 a month. Family account is $14.99 a month for up to six users
Pandora
Basic service with ads is free. Ad-free version (Pandora One) costs $4.99 a month
Spotify
Basic service with ads is free. Ad-free service (Spotify Premium) is $9.99 a month and $4.99 for students. Spotify offers a 50% discount for each person added to a Premium account for up to five users, cutting the cost to $5 per person.
Tidal
$9.99 a month for Tidal Premium or $19.99 for Tidal HiFi, which offers higher-quality sound (both are ad-free). Tidal offers 50% off each person you added to the account for up to five users
